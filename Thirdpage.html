When you first go into Sony Vegas the layout is bunched up with lots of options, but it's not as complicated as it seems.

-The red box, is the explorer window, it is used to locate your files you would like to input into your timeline, you can also find certain FX and transitions.

-The blue box, is the editing window, you can edit single clips in it.

-The green box, is the preview window, you can set the desirable settings so you can preview it, all your work in the timeline will be shown,

The yellow box, is the timeline window, you set all your files there to edit.
